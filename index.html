The Krewe of Vaporwave holds a Virtual Parade each year during Carnival. No two Virtual Parades are the same; each year, VKV changes its approach to the concept and presents the Virtual Parade in a different format.

2018 Virtual Parade - COMING SOON!

The 2018 Virtual Parade - The World’s Unfair is the Krewe of Vaporwave’s reimagination of the 1984 New Orleans World’s Fair, a site-by-site re-imagination of the Fair that will take place within a web-friendly, dungeon crawler-style video game.  The game was released and subsequently un-released due to compatibility issues. We are working on fixes. For now, please consider it thematically appropriate vaporware.

2017 Virtual Parade

The 2017 Virtual Parade is a multiplayer video game that features 20+ floats carrying Vaporwave music videos, with one float representing each year from 1983-2001. It is downloadable directly to your computer and can be accessed anytime.  

If you are the only player in the game when you enter, the parade will not be running. Press Shift+S+P to start the parade.
